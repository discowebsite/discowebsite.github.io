Dr. rer. nat. Paul Nikolaus

Address disco | Distributed Computer Systems Lab
Computer Science Department
TU Kaiserslautern
67663 Kaiserslautern, Germany
Email nikolaus@cs.uni-kl.de

Postdoc at the distributed computer systems lab (DISCO) between April '22 and January '23. Until March '22, PhD student supervised by
Prof. Dr.-Ing. Jens Schmitt (Head of DISCO lab).

Research Interests

  • Network Calculus
  • Performance modeling, analysis, and evaluation of distributed systems
